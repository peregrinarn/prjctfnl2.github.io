ME IN THREE OBJECTS



Hi, I'm René and I want to tell you about some objects that represent me. I consider that volleyball, gardening, music and writing are objects that represent me and identify me as a person.

Volleyball is my favorite sport that I have been practicing for 4 years because I like to work as a team. I like playing volleyball because it makes me competitive, it makes me feel adrenaline and I like to give my best on the court.

Music is essential for me because it helps me concentrate when I do something, music makes me feel calm and motivates me when I need to improve.

The gardening represents me in large part because it gives me peace when I need it and that makes me think and reflect about myself. Gardening is very relaxing for me because it makes me feel close to nature and I like that.

Writing is something very personal for me, I like writing because it helped me vent when I needed it most, it made me improve emotionally. I like to write about repressed feelings that I never expressed and expressed them in fictional stories created by me.
